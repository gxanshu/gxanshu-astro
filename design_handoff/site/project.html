About

I stared at too many rainbow themes. They did nothing for my eyes and less for my focus. So I spent a weekend tuning a palette that felt closer to printed type — warm paper, soft ink, a single amber highlight for what matters.

Design principles

  • One accent only. Warm amber for keywords and strings.
  • Muted neutrals for structure. Punctuation almost invisible.
  • WCAG AA contrast across all syntactic roles.
